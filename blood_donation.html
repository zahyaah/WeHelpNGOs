Do's Don'ts
Eat a healthy meal before donating blood Don't donate blood if you have a cold, flu, or fever
Drink plenty of water before and after donating Don't donate blood if you have recently had a tattoo or piercing
Bring a valid ID Don't donate blood if you have recently traveled to a malaria-endemic area
Wear a short-sleeved shirt or blouse Don't donate blood if you have taken antibiotics or other medications in the past 24 hours
